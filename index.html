"I design AI products as systems — not just screens."

The puzzle that is the human mind and how it behaves is one that is most fascinating. I strive to learn as much as I can about this complex and magical thing then pour all that knowledge into my design work. My passion is to make your passions easier to achieve.

I'm a TCG player with way too many cards, a product designer with 14 years in the industry, and apparently the kind of person who decides to build an entire mobile app alone just because a problem bugged them. I'm currently Manager of Product Experience Design at a global financial services organisation, where I led UX for the company's first GenAI platform.

On the side, I built Deckhunter — a live mobile app on iOS and Android — with Claude as my technical partner. No development team. Users kept writing in saying "hey team" without realising it was just me. That still makes me smile.

I think about AI products as whole systems — the conversation layer, the trust mechanisms, the feedback loops, the operational infrastructure. That's where the design actually happens. I prototype in code, test on real devices with real people, and I care deeply about the details that make AI feel trustworthy rather than just capable.
